ctrl+p open ctrlp fuzzy finder
leader-p ctrl+p

tab autocompletes

:ls show buffer contents (including whether or not a buffer was edited)
:bd<N> closes a buffer

gc comments (gcc comments a whole line)

leader-f toggles nerdtree
s opens buffer in vsp 

leader-b opens buffer list (you can use normal vim searching to search files within buffer)
    - d will kill the buffer
    - ctrl-v vertical splits
    - ctrl-s horizontal splits

ctrl-h/l cycle between open buffers (use the tab "airline")

nerdtree, shift-I toggles dotfiles
    - s is vert split
    - i is hor split
        - not sure why

:scriptnames loads the config files that generate vim settings

:ALEInfo shows ale configs
